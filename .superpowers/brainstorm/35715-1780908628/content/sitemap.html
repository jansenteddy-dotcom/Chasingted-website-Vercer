Chasingted Website — Proposed Page Structure

Based on what you've told me, here are all the pages the new site will need. Let me know if anything is missing or should be removed.

🌍 Public Website (visible to everyone)

Main Pages

  • Homepage — hero, intro, featured trips
  • All Trips — overview of every expedition
  • Trip Detail Page — full info per trip (dates, price, itinerary, photos)
  • Application Form — apply for a specific trip
  • About — story behind Chasingted
  • Contact — contact form / WhatsApp
  • FAQ — common questions

🔐 Customer Portal (login required)

Portal Pages

  • Login / Sign Up — email + password
  • My Trip — trip details, dates, meeting point, packing list
  • Fellow Travelers — profiles of others on the same trip
  • My Profile — personal info, photo, bio
  • Payment Status — deposit paid / balance due (manual for now)

⚙️ Admin Backend (only you, Teddy)

Sanity Studio (your content dashboard)

  • Trips — add, edit, archive trips
  • Applications — review, approve or reject
  • Bookings — mark deposit paid / balance paid
  • Travelers — view profiles per trip
  • Pages — edit About, FAQ, Homepage text

Click any section if you want to flag it for discussion. Otherwise just let me know in the chat what looks right, what's missing, or what should be removed.
