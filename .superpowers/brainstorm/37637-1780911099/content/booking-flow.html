The Full Booking Flow

How a customer goes from finding your site to being in the portal — and what you do at each step.

1
🌍 Visitor browses a trip
They land on a Trip Detail page — see the itinerary, dates, price, and photos. They decide they want to join.
2
📝 They fill in the application form
Name, age, experience level, motivation, emergency contact etc. (exact fields to be confirmed). They hit Submit.
3
✅ Thank You page shown + you get notified
Customer sees confirmation message. You receive an email alert that a new application came in. Application appears in your Sanity admin.
T
🧑‍💼 You review in Sanity Studio (your action)
You open the application, read it, and click Approve or Reject. If rejected, customer gets a polite decline email automatically.
5
📧 Customer gets approval email with bank details
Automatic email confirms their spot is reserved and shows your bank details + deposit amount. They have X days to pay.
T
🧑‍💼 You confirm deposit received (your action)
Once you see the bank transfer, you click "Deposit received" in Sanity Studio. This triggers the next step automatically.
7
🔐 Customer gets portal access
Automatic email with a login link. They create a password, log in, and can now see their trip details, packing list, and fellow travelers' profiles.
8
💰 Balance reminder closer to trip date
System sends a reminder email X weeks before departure. Customer transfers the balance. You mark it paid in Sanity. Portal updates to "Fully paid ✅".
🟠 Orange steps = actions only you take in Sanity Studio
🔵 All other steps = fully automatic — the system handles emails and portal access on its own once you click.
